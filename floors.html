**__Floating Head Form__** Laser -Shoots laser from mouth. Crushing Grip -Summons arm underneath you to crush you. Impending Fist -Summons arm above the player and smashes them. Faster and same attack range as Crushing Grip. Devastating Clap -Summons both arms to clap player. No range indicator. 3+ Second Windup Time. <50% HP: Summons 3 golems surrounding middle of map. Golems don't move from their spot. **__Colossus Form__** Leaping Ground Smash -Boss jumps toward player and causes damage upon landing. Jump length: About 2 backsteps. Arm Sweep -Boss rips off an arm and quickly sweeps around itself twice rapidly. Range: About 2 backsteps. Beat Rush -Boss is locked in one direction, moves foward while swinging fists 5x. Damage: 800+ per hit. Laser Strike -Boss sits down and shoots laser up in the air that later lands upon the target area. Small range indicator upon player's location. Wave Pulse -Boss charges, fistbumps both hands before doing wide AoE Roar. Attack range indicator around the boss.
